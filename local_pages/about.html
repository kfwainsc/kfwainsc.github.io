About Eli

Eli Chisholm is a certified Journeyman Farrier and has been shoeing horses since graduating from Montana State University's Farrier School in 2015. After returning to the Flathead Valley, they continued their education as an apprentice with Jesse Johnston at Glacier Horseshoeing for another __ years, in addition to starting their own farrier service, Pride Hoof Care.

Before becoming a farrier, Eli received a BA from Indiana University Bloomington in Gender Studies, with a minor in Sociology along with an undergraduate honors thesis. Eli loves to snowboard, garden, & backcountry hike. Curling up next to the wood stove with a good book is also a favorite past time.

They reside in 'The Little Steel Box', a shipping container home Eli and their husband Jai built from scractch. This house is also home to two blue heelers Ophilia (aka 'Derpus') & Kybo (alias 'Bread Loaf'). But, no household is complete without a ball python, and his name Marley.
